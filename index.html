Meet Playground Hospitality's Founder

Doug Roth

Doug founded Playground Hospitality to help clients pull together the thousand details that create a successful concept.

As the Playground manager, Doug has partnered with leading corporations to develop new dining concepts, improve existing operations and extend the life span of established restaurants. 

Over his 30-year hospitality career, Doug launched and operated dozens of highly profitable concepts and experienced the occasional stumble. That depth of experience keeps Playground Hospitality clients from making expensive mistakes.

A sought-after industry expert, Doug led the advisory board of the Tulane University Entrepreneurial Hospitality Program and guest lectured at Purdue University.
